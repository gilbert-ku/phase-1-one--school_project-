Admission

Welcome to Three house international school, where we are committed to providing an exceptional education and fostering a love for learning. We are thrilled that you are considering our school for your child's educational journey. Below, you will find all the information you need to understand our admission process and discover what makes Three House International a special place to learn and grow.

Admission Process:

Application: Complete our online application form by [application deadline]. The form includes basic information about your child and requires submission of any necessary documents, such as academic records or recommendation letters. Assessment: Once we receive your application, our admissions team will contact you to schedule an assessment, which may include an interview, academic testing, or a portfolio review. These assessments help us understand your child's strengths, interests, and readiness for our programs. Notification: After careful review of your child's application and assessment results, we will notify you of our admission decision within a week. If your child is accepted, you will receive further instructions for completing the enrollment process.
